adobo

DESCRIPTION

This food is a traditional filipino food often served during holidays

ingredients

  • Pork (pork belly or shoulder)
  • Soy sauce
  • sugar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • waters
  • onion
  • vinigar

steps

  1. Add pork (pork belly or shoulder) to a pot.
  2. Pour in soy sauce, water, vinegar, onion, pepper, salt, and sugar.
  3. Bring everything to a boil over medium heat.
  4. Once boiling, lower the heat and let it simmer.
  5. Do not stir immediately after adding vinegar; let it cook for a few minutes first.
  6. Continue simmering until the pork becomes tender.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Let the sauce reduce until slightly thick and flavorful.
  9. Serve hot with rice.
