Fried Chicken

DESCRIPTION

Fried chicken is a crispy and flavorful dish made by coating chicken with seasoned flour and frying it until golden brown.

ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cooking oil

steps

  1. Wash and prepare the chicken pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix eggs and milk.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Dip each chicken piece into the egg mixture.
  5. Coat the chicken evenly with the flour mixture.
  6. Heat cooking oil in a deep pan.
  7. Carefully place the coated chicken into the hot oil.
  8. Fry until golden brown and fully cooked.
  9. Remove and drain excess oil.
  10. Serve hot.
